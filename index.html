About me

For a long time I have been very passionate about the world of VFX, the simple fact of being able to create something that does not exist or modify my own reality is something that excites me too much.

That passion led me to learn the different programs that allow you to do any type of effect such as Photoshop, Premiere, Blender, etc. And after learning a little about each thing, I am beginning to recognize the areas in which I seek to specialize and are the ones that I want to show you today through my projects.

This project is done in using Stable Diffusion, i used after effects to key the green screen, take frame by frame and put it through stable diffusion to generate the spiderverse style and track only the face, and with the help of Ebysynth using key frames I tracked the clothes, then again in after effects, I did the composition to achieve the style of the film like halftone
